What is PoliticAxes?

PoliticAxes is an expanded political quiz that attempts to assign percentages for seventeen different political axes. You will be presented with a series of statements, and for each, you will answer with your honest opinion, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree.

At the end of the quiz, your scores will be compared against 67 different ideologies to find your closest match. Answer honestly!

There are questions in the test.

What are the 17 axes?

PoliticAxes moves beyond the standard left-right spectrum by measuring 17 distinct dimensions of political and philosophical thought, grouped into four main categories:

GOVERNMENT & SOCIETY

Measures your views on Authority vs. Liberty, Democracy vs. Autocracy, and Federalism vs. Unitary states.

ECONOMICS & LABOR

Tracks views on Markets vs. Equality, and the divide between Collectivism and Individualism.

GLOBAL & MILITARY

Covers Diplomacy, Borders, and Military interventionism, as well as Earth-centric vs. Space-centric goals.

PHILOSOPHY & CULTURE

The "meta" axes: Measuring Nihilism vs. Purpose, Utopianism vs. Realism, and even the level of "Brain Rot" (Meme culture) in your ideology.

What's the "Closest Match" mean?

The quiz uses Euclidean Distance to map your coordinates in a 17-dimensional space against 67 pre-defined ideologies. It finds the ideology with the smallest mathematical distance from your results. This is for educational and entertainment purposes!
